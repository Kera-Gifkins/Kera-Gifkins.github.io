Margins Borders and Padding

Padding

Padding is the space between whatever content could be an image, a video, or just an ordinary paragraph, and the border

Border

The border is the outline around the content, altered by the padding explained above.

Margin

The Margin is fun because this is the only one not affected by the other 2, the margin affects everything else on the page, the margin is the amount of space around the outside of the page, and this applies to both websites and alot of things outside of computers if you look at any book there will be empty space around the text on the pages, this is the books margin and it works exactly the same with webpages
